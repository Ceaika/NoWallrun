# NoWallrunSelfNerf

A client-side mod for Titanfall 2 / Northstar that disables wallrunning.

Ever wanted to play Titanfall like a grounded shooter?
Me neither.

This mod removes one of the core movement mechanics from the game, forcing you to play without wallruns. It’s intended as a self-imposed handicap — whether for practice, challenge, or just to see how it feels.

## What it does

* Prevents wallrunning
* Applies only to the local player
* Does not affect other players

## Purpose

Titanfall is built around movement. This does the opposite.

The idea is simple: remove a major mobility option and see what remains. It changes positioning, engagements, and survivability in ways that are immediately noticeable.

## Installation

Place the mod folder into:

R2Northstar/mods/

## Status

Work in progress.
